About Me

I am a Deep Learning Researcher and Algorithm Engineer with a recently completed Ph.D. in Electrical and Communication Engineering from Shahid Bahonar University. My doctoral work focused primarily on designing novel neural architectures to extract meaningful representations from massive, high-dimensional datasets such as multi-modal Earth Observation data.

I specialize in building and training custom models from scratch—including Vision Transformers (ViT), 3D-CNNs, and ConvLSTMs—using PyTorch. My research tackles complex problems in spatiotemporal reasoning, cross-modal attention mechanisms, and the processing of heterogeneous data lakes.

Beyond theoretical modeling, I have extensive professional experience building high-performance C++ and Python pipelines, utilizing Docker, Git, and deploying reproducible deep learning workflows on multi-GPU HPC clusters and cloud infrastructure.
