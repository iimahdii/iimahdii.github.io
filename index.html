Who Am I ?

About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher and AI Engineer with over 5 years of experience at the Remote Sensing Laboratory, specializing in the intersection of Deep Learning and Earth Observation. My work focuses on developing innovative neural network architectures—including Attention Mechanisms, Transformers (ViT), and 3D-CNNs—to analyze high-dimensional hyperspectral data for precision agriculture and environmental monitoring.

With a strong background in Big Data Engineering and Optimization, I bridge the gap between theoretical AI foundations and practical, scalable applications. I am proficient in Python, TensorFlow, Keras, and Azure ML, and I am passionate about leveraging Geo-AI to solve complex real-world problems.
